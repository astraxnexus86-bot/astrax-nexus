About us

A studio built on craft, in the heart of Limpopo.

AstraX Nexus was founded by Owen Gwaka with a simple idea: Businesses deserve premium digital products — without the premium agency price tag or the throwaway template feel.

We're a small, focused team obsessed with the details. From the first line of code to the final animation, every project is handcrafted and quality-checked.

To help ambitious businesses grow online through beautifully engineered digital experiences, intelligent AI tools, and strategy that actually moves the needle.

◉

Vision

To be Africa's most trusted independent studio for premium websites, web apps, and AI-powered business tools — recognised for quality, warmth, and craft.
